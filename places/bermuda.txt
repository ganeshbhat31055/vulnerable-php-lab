Bermuda is a British Overseas Territory in the North Atlantic Ocean, known for its pink-sand beaches, pastel-colored houses, and the mysterious Bermuda Triangle. Offers a unique blend of British and Caribbean culture. The island's capital, Hamilton, features colonial architecture and a vibrant shopping scene. The crystal-clear waters are perfect for snorkeling and diving, with numerous shipwrecks and coral reefs to explore. The local cuisine combines British and Caribbean influences, with specialties like fish chowder and codfish breakfast. The island's history is rich with maritime heritage, visible in its museums and historic sites. Despite its small size, Bermuda offers diverse landscapes from bustling towns to secluded coves and nature reserves.
