Boracay is a small island in the Philippines known for its white sandy beaches, vibrant nightlife, and water sports. Its White Beach is consistently ranked among the best beaches in the world, stretching over 4 kilometers of powdery white sand. The island offers a perfect mix of relaxation and entertainment, with beachfront bars, restaurants, and shops lining the shore. Water activities include parasailing, scuba diving, and kiteboarding. After a major rehabilitation effort in 2018, Boracay has emerged as a model for sustainable tourism, with strict environmental regulations ensuring the preservation of its natural beauty while maintaining its status as a world-class destination.
