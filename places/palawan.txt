Palawan is a pristine island province in the Philippines, famous for its dramatic limestone cliffs, crystal-clear waters, and diverse marine ecosystem. Home to the UNESCO-listed Puerto Princesa Underground River, one of the New 7 Wonders of Nature. The island offers a perfect blend of adventure and relaxation, with activities ranging from island hopping in El Nido to diving in Coron's World War II shipwrecks. The local cuisine features fresh seafood and tropical fruits, while the indigenous culture adds a unique dimension to the visitor experience. The island's commitment to sustainable tourism has helped preserve its natural beauty and biodiversity.
