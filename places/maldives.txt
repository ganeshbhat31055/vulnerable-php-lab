Maldives is a tropical paradise in the Indian Ocean, known for its crystal-clear waters, overwater bungalows, and vibrant marine life. The Maldives is a collection of 26 atolls with more than 1,000 coral islands. The islands are famous for their luxury resorts, each occupying its own island, and offering unparalleled privacy and exclusivity. The underwater world is a paradise for divers and snorkelers, with colorful coral reefs and diverse marine life including manta rays, whale sharks, and sea turtles. The local culture is a unique blend of South Asian, Arab, and African influences, visible in the music, dance, and cuisine.
